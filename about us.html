    Our Mission

    At IDM, our mission is to provide top-quality, reliable, and eco-friendly cleaning services tailored to our clients’ needs. We are committed to maintaining spotless and healthy spaces through professionalism, attention to detail, and the use of safe, sustainable cleaning solutions. Our goal is to exceed expectations, build lasting relationships, and create a cleaner, happier world—one space at a time.

    Our Vision

    To be the leading cleaning service provider, transforming spaces with excellence, reliability, and eco-friendly solutions. We strive to create healthier environments, enhance well-being, and set the standard for quality and professionalism in the cleaning industry.

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    What is your return policy?

    We offer a 30-day return policy on all our products. Please contact us for further instructions.

    How long does shipping take?

    Shipping typically takes 5-7 business days, depending on your location.

    Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we ship internationally. Additional shipping fees may apply.

    Can I change my order after placing it?

    Once an order is placed, it cannot be changed. However, you can cancel it within the first 24 hours.
