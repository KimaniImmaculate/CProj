Our Mission

At IDM, our mission is to provide top-quality, reliable, and eco-friendly cleaning services tailored to our clients’ needs. We are committed to maintaining spotless and healthy spaces through professionalism, attention to detail, and the use of safe, sustainable cleaning solutions. Our goal is to exceed expectations, build lasting relationships, and create a cleaner, happier world—one space at a time.

Our Vision

To be the leading cleaning service provider, transforming spaces with excellence, reliability, and eco-friendly solutions. We strive to create healthier environments, enhance well-being, and set the standard for quality and professionalism in the cleaning industry.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Are your cleaning products safe?

Yes, we use eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for both your family and pets.

How do I book a cleaning service?

Booking a cleaning service is simple! Just visit our booking page or call us to schedule your appointment. We’ll confirm the details and have our team ready to clean your space.

How long will my cleaning appointment take?

The duration of the cleaning depends on the size of the area and the type of service requested. Typically, a standard cleaning takes around 2-4 hours.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, you do not need to be home. You can leave the keys with us, and our professional cleaners will take care of everything. However, we will need to discuss access details beforehand.

Can I customize my cleaning service?

Yes, we offer customizable cleaning packages to suit your needs. You can choose specific areas or items to be cleaned, and we will tailor the service accordingly.

What should I do before the cleaning team arrives?

Please ensure that all personal items are stored away, and any specific cleaning instructions are communicated to our team prior to the appointment. This helps us perform a thorough and efficient cleaning job.
